SIR. AHMADU BELLO

The man who masterminded the independence of Nigeria

Here's a timeline of sir Ahmadus life:

  • 1910- Born in Rabah, Sokoto
  • 1931- Finished sokoto provincial college and subsequently became english master in sokoto middle school
  • 1934- Bello was made the district head of Rabah by sultan Hassan dan Muazu succeeding his brother
  • 1938- He was promoted to the position of divisional head of Gusau
  • 1938- Bello made attempt to become the sultan of sokoto but was not successful
  • 1939- The new sultan made sir Ahmadu Bello the sardauna(crown prince) of sokoto a chieftancy title and promoted him to the sokoto authority council
  • 1940- He joined jamiyya mutanen arewa which will later become the northen peoples congress (NPC)
  • 1948- He traveled to england on a government scholarship to study local government administration which broadened his understanding & knowledge of governance
  • 1952- sir Ahmadu won a seat in the northen house of assembly
  • 1954- Bello became the first premier of northen nigeria
  • 1959- Bello led the NPC to win a plurality of the parliamentry seats
  • 1960- in forming the 1960 independence federal government of the nigeria, Bello as president of the NPC chose to remain premier of northen nigeria
  • 1966- Bello was assassinated by major Chukwuma Kaduna Nzeogwo
